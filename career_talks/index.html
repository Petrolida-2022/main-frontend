About Petrolida Career Talks

Petrolida Career Talk 2022 is a virtual event filled with workshop series and talk shows that bring a journey to light up your way towards an excellent career. This event will be filled with experienced speakers and experts in their fields to discuss and help you level up your career preparation.

Petrolida Career Talk 2022 will be open for university students all over Indonesia in order to provide insight, awareness, information, and education about career preparation so that in the future the participants are ready to enter the working field.

Nail an Astounding First Impression

Date March 12th 2022

10.00 AM - 11.45 AM

Curriculum vitae are one vital section in job hunting considering they are your first and maybe only direct contact with a potential employer. For this reason, a CV should be meticulously planned, structured, and written in such a way that it put a great impact on the reader. Through Petrolida Career Talks, we provide a platform that helps our audience to be able to create fine quality CVs that reflect them as a person.
